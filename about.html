Scouting at MNCHS

MNCHS Senior Scouts Outfit 002 is the Boy Scouts of the Philippines Organization at Masbate National Comprehensive High School.

BSP MISSION 2025

To help the youth develop values and acquire competencies to become responsible leaders and capable anchored on the Scout Oath and Law.

BSP VISION 2025

Foremost in preparing the youth to become agents of change in communities guided by the Scout Oath and Law.

Years of Excellence

Established in 1954, Outfit 002 has produced countless leaders, community servants, and achievers. Our legacy continues with the Class of 2026!
